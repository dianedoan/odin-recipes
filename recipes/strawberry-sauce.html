Strawberry Sauce

Description

A simple strawberry sauce using fresh strawberries and requires no cooking.

Original recipe obtained here.

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts of fresh strawberries that are cleaned and sliced
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar

Steps

  1. Put the strawberries in a large bowl.
  2. Sprinkle sugar evenly over the strawberries and stir until evenly coated.
  3. Let it stand at room temperature while stirring ocacasionally for 10 minutes.
  4. Cover the bowl and chill in the fridge until it is ready to serve.
